PureQR – Privacy Policy
Last updated: March 18, 2026

PureQR is committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed to operate 100% offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

1. No Data Collection
   PureQR does not collect, store, share, or process any personal information.
   The app does not use:
   - Analytics
   - Tracking technologies
   - Advertising SDKs
   - Crash reporting services
   - External servers or cloud services
   
   All QR code generation and scanning happens locally on your device.

2. No Internet Access
   PureQR does not request or use internet permissions.
   The app cannot send or receive data over the network.

3. Camera Access (New)
   When you use the Scanner feature:
   - The app requests permission to use your device's camera.
   - The camera is used solely to scan and decode QR codes and barcodes in real-time.
   - Processing is performed locally on your device using the Google ML Kit library.
   - PureQR does NOT capture, store, or transmit any images, videos, or scanned data to any external servers.
   - The camera is only active while the Scanner screen is open.

4. Contact Access (Optional)
   If you choose to import a contact to generate a vCard QR code:
   - The app uses the system contact picker.
   - You select the contact manually.
   - PureQR receives temporary access only to the chosen contact.
   - The data is used solely to generate a QR code.
   - The data is not stored, not uploaded, and not shared.
   
   PureQR never accesses your full contact list.

5. File Access
   When saving PNG or PDF files:
   - Files are stored locally on your device.
   - PureQR does not access any other files.
   - No file data is transmitted or collected.
   
   On Android 10+, the app uses scoped storage and does not require broad file permissions.

6. Children’s Privacy
   PureQR does not collect any data from users of any age, including children.

7. Changes to This Policy
   If this privacy policy is updated, the new version will be posted at the same URL.
