Privacy isn't a feature,
it's our foundation.

Most QR scanners send your data to remote servers for "analysis." Pure-QR does everything locally. No internet permission is even requested.

  • 100% Offline Processing
  • No Analytics or Tracking
  • Zero External SDKs for Ads

Payload Inspector

Automatically identify tracking parameters (UTM, affiliate IDs) before you open any link.
